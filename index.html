What these columns mean

Reliability is a 0 to 100 score for how dependable the player looks based on playing time, availability, and data confidence.

Per 90 estimates fantasy points if the player played a full 90 minutes.

Risk is a 0 to 100 score where lower is better. It combines injury/status risk, minutes risk, card risk, weekly volatility, and bad-week risk.
